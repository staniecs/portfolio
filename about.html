Lucas Staniec

Concept Artist located in Poland

Lucas Staniec is a concept artist and illustrator working in the game industry. He is currently working as a freelance concept artist for various clients. He has previously collaborated with Karakter Design Studio, and has worked on Cyberpunk 2077 and Dying Light 2. Other titles include: Magic the Gathering, Warhammer Total War 2 series, Arkham Horror, Star Wars and Lord of the Rings card games, Blair Witch computer game, and many more
